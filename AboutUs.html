Our Mission

To empower Cape Town's youth by providing them with opportunities for personal growth, skill development, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A Cape Town where every young person has the opportunity to thrive, irrespective of their socioeconomic background.

Our Story

Founded in 2024, CTYEH was born out of the need to address the challenges faced by underprivileged youth in Cape Town. Through various programs, we aim to create a brighter future for these young individuals.
